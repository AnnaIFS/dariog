The Work

The work looks different for each person.

The direction is always the same: back to your Self.

The Approach

I work at the intersection of two things that are usually kept separate. Modern psychology - specifically Internal Family Systems, or IFS - and twenty years of practice rooted in the Sumak Kawsay tradition.

IFS works with the inner system. The idea is simple: we are not one thing. We carry many parts - parts that protect, parts that carry old pain, parts that know more than we allow them to speak. When those parts are understood and embraced rather than fought, something shifts.

The traditional practice brings something else. Presence. Rhythm. The body. The land. The kind of knowing that does not come from thinking.

Together, these two approaches reach places that neither reaches alone.

I work one-on-one, with couples, and in groups. I also work with organizations through FINO. The format changes. The purpose does not.

Sessions are available in English, Spanish, Italian, and Portuguese.

Group Work

Some work can only happen in a group. Being witnessed by others who are doing the same thing changes what becomes possible. The circle holds something that one-on-one work cannot.

Group offerings include circles, vision quests, and multi-day retreats in nature. They draw on IFS, psychology, and practice rooted in the Sumak Kawsay tradition. They are not workshops or seminars. They are experiences that ask something of you.

Vision quests are ancient rituals held by people all over the world. Time alone in nature, fasting from word, food, and water, supported by the elements and by the community that holds the space. People go back to what is real, what is timeless, and bring their transformation back with them.

These gatherings happen in person, across multiple days. The community that forms continues after the experience ends.
